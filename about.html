About Peer Finder

What is this?

Peer Finder is a simple demo that shows how students can be matched for group projects based on their skills and interests.

How does matching work?

The system scores profiles based on:

  • Education Level: Must match your level
  • Complementary Skills: Different skills that complement yours
  • Shared Interests: Same project subject preferences

Matching Logic Explained

Our algorithm prioritizes complementary skills over shared skills. Here's why:

Design Philosophy

Complementary Skills = 7 points each

Shared Skills = 5 points each

A good project partner is someone who complements you more than mirrors you. When team members have different but compatible skills, they create a more balanced and capable team. For example, if you're strong in programming, partnering with someone skilled in design or marketing creates a more complete project team.
